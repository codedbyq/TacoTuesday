Taco Tuesday

It's Taco Tuuuuuesday! Taco Tuesday is a food-themed matching game where you will take the role of a chef running their own taco truck. Get ready as you take on orders from hungry customers. You must match the order exactly as it came in. And most importantly... The people love their tacos so be sure that you don't keep 'em waiting!
